Grave Harvest

styczeń 2023

Rodzaj: 2D

Team Size: 6

Game Engine: Godot

Dration: Made in 3 days during Ludum Dare 53 game jam

Have you ever dreamt of becoming a pumpkin farmer? No? Doesn't matter, because now you can become one! You're only two clicks away from fulfilling this dream! Don't wait, just jump in the mysterious far... graveyard and start growing the biggest pumpkin of all time! The most elite contest for the greatest pumpkin in the universe won't win itself, will it? Make the biggest pumpkin but feeding it with fertilizer from task (repairing cross, lighting a candle, planting flowers, cutting grass). But be aware of ghost and the time passing
